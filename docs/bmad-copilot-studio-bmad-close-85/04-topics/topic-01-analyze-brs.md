# Topic 01 — Analyze BRS

## Trigger phrases

- Analyze this BRS
- Review this business requirements document
- Extract facts and questions
- Start BMAD handoff

## Goal

Produce a structured discovery analysis from the BRS and source materials.

## Steps

1. Identify source documents.
2. Extract business problem, goals, actors, systems, scope, constraints.
3. Separate confirmed facts, inferred requirements, assumptions, and open questions.
4. Identify risks and dependencies.
5. Recommend next topic.

## Output

Use `06-output-templates/01-project-brief-template.md` as the target shape for summary content.
