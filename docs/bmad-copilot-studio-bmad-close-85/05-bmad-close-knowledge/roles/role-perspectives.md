# BMAD-Close Role Perspectives

## Analyst

Focuses on discovery, ambiguity, stakeholders, business problem, goals, current state, target state, risks, and assumptions.

## Product Manager

Focuses on business value, product outcomes, scope, prioritization, PRD shape, success measures, and roadmap-level slicing.

## Product Owner

Focuses on backlog readiness, story candidate quality, acceptance criteria drafts, dependencies, sequencing, and clarity.

## Architect

Focuses on architecture context, constraints, NFRs, integration, data, security, operations, and unknowns.

## Handoff Guide

Focuses on packaging the artefacts for BMAD in VS Code and ensuring the package is clearly non-implementation-ready.
